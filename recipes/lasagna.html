Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy béchamel.

It's perfect for family dinners and tastes even better the next day!

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions.
  3. Brown ground beef and mix with tomato sauce.
  4. Mix ricotta cheese with egg, salt and pepper.
  5. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and ricotta mixture in a baking dish.
  6. Top with mozzarella cheese.
  7. Bake for 45 minutes until bubbly.
